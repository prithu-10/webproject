Our Story

Discover the passion and tradition behind Okkhar Restaurant

Welcome to Okkhar

Founded in 2010, Okkhar Restaurant brings the authentic flavors of Nepal to the heart of Kathmandu. Our journey began with a simple vision: to create a dining experience that celebrates traditional recipes while embracing modern culinary techniques.

At Okkhar, we believe in the power of food to bring people together. Our chefs meticulously prepare each dish using the finest ingredients, many imported directly from Nepal to ensure authentic flavors.

With two locations in Nepal- Kathmandu- Pokhara we've grown from a small family restaurant to one of the city's most beloved dining destinations, earning recognition for our exceptional cuisine and warm hospitality.
